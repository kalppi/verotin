FINNISH TAX DEDUCTION RULES AND REGULATIONS - 2025
Classifier-oriented version

PURPOSE
=======
These rules are for categorizing Finnish invoice lines, receipts and expenses
into likely tax deduction categories.

The goal is NOT only to describe deductions, but to prevent false positives.
Each category includes:
- positive signals
- hard exclusions
- classification priority
- examples

MANDATORY CLASSIFICATION PROCESS
================================

STEP 0: Identify expense domain
-------------------------------
Every expense MUST first be assigned to one primary domain:

A) Household / private home / holiday home
B) Work / income acquisition / business
C) Vehicle / transport
D) Business travel
E) Membership / insurance
F) Unclear / needs manual review

Domain rules:
- If the line concerns installation, repair, renovation, maintenance or
  improvement of a home, apartment, yard, building, heating system, cooling
  system, electricity system, plumbing, solar panels or heat pump:
  → classify domain as Household.

- If the line concerns tools, devices, software, books, training or work
  equipment used for earning income:
  → classify domain as Work / income acquisition / business.

- If the line explicitly concerns a car, vehicle, fuel, charging, mileage,
  tyres, maintenance of a vehicle, parking, leasing or vehicle insurance:
  → classify domain as Vehicle / transport.

- If the line concerns accommodation, flights, trains, taxis, business-trip
  meals, travel tickets or temporary work trips:
  → classify domain as Business travel.

- If the line concerns professional insurance, unemployment fund fees or
  professional memberships:
  → classify domain as Membership / insurance.

- If the line is too generic:
  → classify as Unclear / needs manual review.

STEP 1: Apply priority rules
----------------------------
Priority order:

1. Kotitalousvähennys
2. Vehicle / transport expenses
3. Travel expenses
4. Tools and equipment
5. Training and professional development
6. Insurance and memberships
7. Home office deduction
8. Meals and hospitality
9. Depreciation
10. Unclear / manual review

Important:
- If work is performed on a private home or holiday home, prefer
  Kotitalousvähennys over work/business categories.
- If the item is a vehicle expense, prefer Vehicle over Travel unless it is
  clearly part of a temporary business trip.

1. KOTITALOUSVÄHENNYS
=====================

Description:
Tax credit for household, care, maintenance, installation and renovation work
performed at home or holiday home.

Strong positive signals:
- asennus, perusasennus
- huolto, korjaus, remontti
- siivous, hoivatyö
- piha, puutarha, lumityö
- ilmalämpöpumppu, lämpöpumppu
- sähkötyö, putkityö, aurinkopaneeli

Deductible:
- Labour portion only

Not deductible:
- Materials
- Devices (e.g. heat pump itself)
- Travel costs
- New construction

2025 deduction:
- 60% of labour when purchased from a company
- maximum €3,500 per person
- own-risk €100

Oil heating replacement:
- 60% labour
- max €3,500

Hard exclusions:
- Never classify as vehicle or tools

Example:
"ilmalämpöpumpun perusasennus"
→ Kotitalousvähennys (labour only)

2. HOME OFFICE DEDUCTIONS (Työhuonevähennys)
============================================

Description:
Deduction for use of workspace at home.

2025 rules (employees allowed):
Standard deduction amounts:
- €980: full-time use
- €490: part-time use
- €245: occasional use

Positive signals:
- työhuone
- kotitoimisto
- etätyötila

Deductible:
- Workspace usage (flat-rate or actual costs)

Hard exclusions:
- Not for installations or renovations
- Not for equipment purchases

Example:
"työhuonevähennys"
→ Home office deduction

3. OFFICE EQUIPMENT AND SUPPLIES (Työvälineet)
==============================================

Description:
Work tools and equipment used to earn income.

Positive signals:
- tietokone, näyttö, työtuoli
- ohjelmisto, lisenssi
- toimistotarvike

Rules:
- < €1,200 → immediate deduction
- > €1,200 → depreciation

Hard exclusions:
- Not home renovation
- Not vehicle-related

Example:
"näyttö työkäyttöön"
→ Tools

4. PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND TRAINING
========================================

Positive signals:
- kurssi, koulutus, seminaari

Deductible:
- Training related to current profession

Not deductible:
- New profession training
- Hobby courses

5. VEHICLE AND FUEL EXPENSES
=============================

Required signals:
- auto, polttoaine, km, huolto (vehicle context)

2025 reference:
- €0.57/km

Hard exclusions:
- Not home installations
- Not generic "asennus"

Example:
"auton huolto"
→ Vehicle

6. MEALS AND HOSPITALITY
========================

Deductible:
- Business meals
- Travel meals

Reference:
- full allowance ~€53
- partial ~€24
- meal allowance ~€13

7. TRAVEL EXPENSES
==================

Deductible:
- Flights, trains, hotels

Commuting:
- Max €7,000
- Own-risk €900

8. INSURANCE AND MEMBERSHIP
===========================

Deductible:
- Professional insurance
- Unemployment fund
- Trade union fees (STILL deductible in 2025)

9. DEPRECIATION
===============

Rules:
- > €1,200 assets depreciated
- Small assets deductible immediately

10. DOCUMENTATION
=================

Keep:
- receipts
- proof of payment
- usage explanation
- mileage logs

CLASSIFICATION EXAMPLES
=======================

"ilmalämpöpumpun perusasennus"
→ Kotitalousvähennys

"ilmalämpöpumppu"
→ Not deductible (equipment)

"auton huolto"
→ Vehicle

"näyttö"
→ Tools

"työhuonevähennys"
→ Home office (allowed in 2025)

"ammattiliiton jäsenmaksu"
→ Deductible (2025 only)
