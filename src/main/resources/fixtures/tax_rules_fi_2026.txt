FINNISH TAX DEDUCTION RULES AND REGULATIONS - 2026
Classifier-oriented version

PURPOSE
=======
These rules are for categorizing Finnish invoice lines, receipts and expenses
into likely tax deduction categories.

The goal is NOT only to describe deductions, but to prevent false positives.
Each category includes:
- positive signals
- hard exclusions
- classification priority
- examples

MANDATORY CLASSIFICATION PROCESS
================================

STEP 0: Identify expense domain
-------------------------------
Every expense MUST first be assigned to one primary domain:

A) Household / private home / holiday home
B) Work / income acquisition / business
C) Vehicle / transport
D) Business travel
E) Membership / insurance
F) Unclear / needs manual review

Domain rules:
- If the line concerns installation, repair, renovation, maintenance or
  improvement of a home, apartment, yard, building, heating system, cooling
  system, electricity system, plumbing, solar panels or heat pump:
  → classify domain as Household.

- If the line concerns tools, devices, software, books, training or work
  equipment used for earning income:
  → classify domain as Work / income acquisition / business.

- If the line explicitly concerns a car, vehicle, fuel, charging, mileage,
  tyres, maintenance of a vehicle, parking, leasing or vehicle insurance:
  → classify domain as Vehicle / transport.

- If the line concerns accommodation, flights, trains, taxis, business-trip
  meals, travel tickets or temporary work trips:
  → classify domain as Business travel.

- If the line concerns professional insurance, unemployment fund fees or
  professional memberships:
  → classify domain as Membership / insurance.

- If the line is too generic, for example "service", "work", "installation",
  or "fee" without object:
  → classify as Unclear / needs manual review.

STEP 1: Apply priority rules
----------------------------
Priority order:

1. Kotitalousvähennys
2. Vehicle / transport expenses
3. Travel expenses
4. Tools and equipment
5. Training and professional development
6. Insurance and memberships
7. Home office deduction
8. Meals and hospitality
9. Depreciation
10. Unclear / manual review

Important:
- If work is performed on a private home or holiday home, prefer
  Kotitalousvähennys over work/business categories.
- If the item is a vehicle expense, prefer Vehicle over Travel unless it is
  clearly part of a temporary business trip.
- Do NOT classify based on one generic word only. "Asennus" alone is not enough;
  the object of installation matters.

1. KOTITALOUSVÄHENNYS
=====================

Description:
Kotitalousvähennys is a tax credit for eligible household, care, maintenance,
installation and renovation work performed at a taxpayer's home or holiday home.

Strong positive signals:
- kotitalousvähennys
- koti
- asunto
- omakotitalo
- mökki
- vapaa-ajan asunto
- piha
- remontti
- korjaus
- kunnossapito
- huolto
- asennus
- perusasennus
- saneeraus
- siivous
- hoivatyö
- hoitotyö
- pihatyö
- lumityö
- puutarhatyö

Home system / renovation signals:
- ilmalämpöpumppu
- lämpöpumppu
- maalämpö
- lämmitys
- ilmastointi
- ilmanvaihto
- sähkötyö
- putkityö
- vesijohto
- viemäri
- aurinkopaneeli
- öljylämmitys
- öljylämmityksestä luopuminen

Deductible:
- Labour portion of eligible work.
- Work bought from a company in the prepayment register.
- Direct wages and employer side costs if hiring a person directly.

Not deductible:
- Materials
- Equipment
- Devices
- Travel costs
- Freight / delivery
- Inspection-only work
- New construction
- Work not done at home or holiday home
- Work where the invoice does not separate labour from materials

2026 deduction amounts:
- Standard household, care and renovation work:
  - 35% of VAT-included labour cost when bought from a company
  - maximum €1,600 per person per year
  - own-risk amount €150 per person per year

- Oil heating replacement:
  - 60% of VAT-included labour cost when bought from a company
  - maximum €3,500 per person per year
  - own-risk amount €150 per person per year

Hard exclusions:
- Do NOT classify as Vehicle just because the line contains "pump", "engine",
  "installation" or "maintenance".
- Do NOT classify as Home office just because the work happens at home.
- Do NOT classify heat pump, solar panel, plumbing, electrical or renovation
  work as office equipment.

Classifier examples:
- "ilmalämpöpumpun perusasennus"
  → Kotitalousvähennys
  → Reason: heat pump + installation = home improvement labour
  → Deductible only if labour portion is separated

- "ilmalämpöpumppu"
  → Not directly deductible as kotitalousvähennys
  → Reason: device/equipment, not labour

- "ilmalämpöpumpun huolto"
  → Kotitalousvähennys candidate
  → Reason: maintenance work on home system

- "aurinkopaneelien asennus"
  → Kotitalousvähennys
  → Reason: installation work on home energy system

- "remonttitarvikkeet"
  → Not deductible as kotitalousvähennys
  → Reason: materials

2. HOME OFFICE DEDUCTIONS (Työhuonevähennys)
============================================

Description:
Työhuonevähennys concerns the use of a room or workspace for income acquisition.

Important 2026 employee rule:
- Employees can no longer claim the home office deduction from wage income
  for working at home or at a holiday home.
- Employees still receive the automatic income-acquisition deduction where
  applicable, but home-office deduction itself is not available for 2026
  wage income.

For non-wage income:
Standard home office deductions may still apply:
- €980: continuous full-time use, or working at home more than half of workdays
- €490: continuous part-time use, or 25–50% of workdays
- €245: occasional but repeated work use

Positive signals:
- työhuone
- kotitoimisto
- etätyötila
- työtila
- workspace
- office room
- room used for work

Deductible:
- Use of workspace for income acquisition, where allowed.
- Actual workspace expenses if properly documented and directly related.

Hard exclusions:
- Do NOT classify home repairs, heating, plumbing, electrical work or heat pump
  installation as Työhuonevähennys.
- Do NOT classify furniture or devices automatically as Työhuonevähennys;
  use Tools and equipment instead.
- Do NOT classify employee remote-work costs as Työhuonevähennys for 2026
  wage income.

Classifier examples:
- "työhuonevähennys"
  → Home office deduction

- "kotitoimiston vuokraosuus"
  → Home office deduction candidate

- "ilmalämpöpumpun perusasennus"
  → NOT Home office deduction
  → Use Kotitalousvähennys

3. OFFICE EQUIPMENT AND SUPPLIES (Työvälineet)
==============================================

Description:
Tools, devices and equipment needed for work or income acquisition.

Positive signals:
- työväline
- tietokone
- näyttö
- näppäimistö
- hiiri
- työtuoli
- työpöytä
- ohjelmisto
- lisenssi
- kuulokkeet
- kamera
- mikrofoni
- tulostin
- toimistotarvike
- office equipment
- work equipment

Deductible:
- Work tools and equipment used to earn income.
- Maintenance and repair of work tools.
- Computer and telecommunications costs if used for work.

2026 threshold:
- If a work tool costs less than €1,200, it can usually be deducted fully
  in the year of purchase.
- If the item costs more than €1,200 and has a useful life over 3 years,
  it is usually depreciated over time.

Computer / internet use:
- 0% if there is no evidence of work use
- 50% if there is evidence of work use
- 100% if mainly used for work or for significant side income

Hard exclusions:
- Do NOT classify home renovation, heat pump installation, plumbing,
  electrical work or other home system work as office equipment.
- Do NOT classify vehicle parts as office equipment.
- Do NOT classify training as equipment.

Classifier examples:
- "näyttö työkäyttöön"
  → Tools and equipment

- "kannettava tietokone"
  → Tools and equipment

- "ilmalämpöpumpun perusasennus"
  → NOT Tools and equipment
  → Use Kotitalousvähennys if labour at home

4. PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND TRAINING (Koulutus)
===================================================

Description:
Training costs may be deductible when they maintain or develop existing
professional competence.

Positive signals:
- koulutus
- kurssi
- seminaari
- konferenssi
- sertifikaatti
- workshop
- webinaari
- ammattikirjallisuus
- professional training
- certification

Deductible:
- Courses related to current work or business
- Professional certifications
- Seminars and conferences
- Professional books and publications
- Online learning platforms, if directly work-related

Not deductible:
- Initial education for a new profession
- Hobby courses
- General personal development without work connection
- Training unrelated to current income acquisition

Hard exclusions:
- Do NOT classify installation, repair, tools, travel tickets or meals as
  training unless the invoice clearly concerns training.

Classifier examples:
- "AWS certification exam"
  → Training / professional development

- "React kurssi"
  → Training candidate if related to current work

5. VEHICLE AND FUEL EXPENSES (Polttoaine ja ajoneuvokulut)
==========================================================

Description:
Vehicle expenses concern vehicles and driving for business or income-producing
purposes.

Required vehicle signals:
At least one direct vehicle/transport signal should be present:
- auto
- ajoneuvo
- pakettiauto
- kuorma-auto
- moottoripyörä
- polttoaine
- bensiini
- diesel
- lataus
- sähköauton lataus
- renkaat
- katsastus
- huolto, when clearly vehicle maintenance
- autovakuutus
- pysäköinti
- kilometrikorvaus
- km
- leasing

Deductible:
- Fuel or charging for business trips
- Vehicle maintenance and repairs for business-use portion
- Insurance for business-use portion
- Parking for business trips
- Depreciation or leasing for business-use portion
- Mileage-based deductions where applicable

2026 reference:
- Tax-free kilometre allowance for car use: €0.55/km

Recordkeeping:
- Mileage log
- Date
- Route
- Distance
- Business purpose
- Separation of business and private use

Hard exclusions:
- Do NOT classify home installation, heat pump installation, renovation,
  plumbing or electrical work as vehicle expense.
- Do NOT classify "perusasennus" as vehicle expense unless the object is
  explicitly a vehicle part or vehicle accessory.
- Do NOT classify "pumppu" as vehicle expense unless it clearly means a
  vehicle fuel pump, water pump or similar vehicle component.

Classifier examples:
- "diesel työajo"
  → Vehicle and fuel

- "auton huolto"
  → Vehicle and fuel

- "ilmalämpöpumpun perusasennus"
  → NOT Vehicle
  → Use Kotitalousvähennys

6. MEALS AND HOSPITALITY (Ruoka ja vieraanvaraisuus)
====================================================

Description:
Meals may be deductible when they are linked to business travel, client meetings
or income acquisition.

Positive signals:
- lounas
- päivällinen
- ravintola
- asiakastapaaminen
- edustus
- meal
- lunch
- dinner
- hospitality
- catering

Deductible:
- Business meals with documented business purpose
- Meals during business travel
- Client/partner meals where attendees and purpose are documented

2026 travel allowance references:
- Full domestic daily allowance: €54
- Partial domestic daily allowance: €25
- Meal allowance: €13.50

Hard exclusions:
- Personal meals are not deductible.
- Groceries for private use are not deductible.
- Alcohol or entertainment may be restricted.
- Do NOT classify training fees, travel tickets or tools as meals.

Classifier examples:
- "lounas asiakaspalaveri"
  → Meals and hospitality candidate

- "hotelliaamiainen työmatkalla"
  → Travel-related meal candidate

7. TRAVEL EXPENSES (Matkakulut)
===============================

Description:
Travel expenses concern temporary work trips or business trips.

Positive signals:
- lento
- juna
- bussi
- taksi
- hotelli
- majoitus
- työmatka
- business trip
- travel
- accommodation
- flight
- train ticket

Deductible:
- Accommodation during business travel
- Flights, trains, buses and taxis for business trips
- Car rental for business use
- Travel-related meals according to travel rules

Commuting:
- Normal commuting between home and regular workplace is not business travel.
- For employee commuting deductions in 2026:
  - Maximum deduction: €7,000
  - Own-risk amount: €900

Hard exclusions:
- Do NOT classify vehicle maintenance as travel unless it is clearly a direct
  cost of a specific business trip.
- Do NOT classify home installation work as travel.
- Do NOT classify normal commuting as business travel.

Classifier examples:
- "juna Helsinki-Tampere asiakastapaaminen"
  → Travel expenses

- "hotelli konferenssimatka"
  → Travel expenses

8. INSURANCE AND PROFESSIONAL MEMBERSHIP
========================================

Description:
Professional and business-related insurance and some memberships may be
deductible.

Positive signals:
- vastuuvakuutus
- yrittäjän vakuutus
- laitevakuutus
- omaisuusvakuutus
- työttömyyskassa
- ammattijärjestö
- professional association
- liability insurance

Deductible:
- Professional liability insurance
- Equipment/property insurance for business use
- Business-related professional association fees
- Unemployment fund fees

Important 2026 rule:
- Trade union / labour market organisation membership fees are no longer
  deductible from 1 January 2026.
- Unemployment fund fees remain deductible.

Hard exclusions:
- Private life insurance and private health insurance are generally not
  deductible as work expenses.
- Do NOT classify general service fees or training as membership/insurance
  without explicit membership or insurance wording.

Classifier examples:
- "työttömyyskassan jäsenmaksu"
  → Insurance / membership, deductible

- "ammattiliiton jäsenmaksu"
  → Not deductible from 2026

9. DEPRECIATION (Poistot)
=========================

Description:
Depreciation applies to larger assets that are used for income acquisition
over several years.

Positive signals:
- käyttöomaisuus
- kalusto
- kone
- laite
- investointi
- poisto
- depreciation
- asset

Rules:
- Small work tools under €1,200 can usually be deducted immediately.
- Larger assets are usually depreciated over time if useful life exceeds
  3 years.
- Business machinery and equipment are commonly depreciated annually from
  remaining balance.
- Computers and work equipment are often treated under tools/equipment first.

Sole trader small acquisition rule:
- Small acquisitions up to €1,200 each can be deducted immediately.
- Total small acquisition deduction limit: €3,600 per year.

Hard exclusions:
- Do NOT use depreciation for kotitalousvähennys labour.
- Do NOT depreciate private home renovation as business equipment.
- Do NOT classify consumables as depreciation.

Classifier examples:
- "työkone 2 500 euroa"
  → Depreciation candidate

- "laptop 900 euroa"
  → Tools and equipment, immediate deduction candidate

10. HOME OFFICE AREA CALCULATION
================================

Use only when actual workspace costs are relevant and allowed.

Do NOT use this calculation for:
- Employee wage-income home office deduction in 2026
- Heat pump installation
- Home renovation work
- Furniture or work devices
- Kotitalousvähennys labour

Example:
Home size: 100 m²
Dedicated income-producing workspace: 20 m²
Workspace percentage: 20 / 100 = 20%
Eligible annual housing costs: €10,000
Potential business-use share: €2,000

Actual-cost deductions require documentation and must be justifiable.

11. IMPORTANT DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS
========================================

For all deductions, keep:
- Original receipts and invoices
- Proof of payment
- Business/work purpose documentation
- Mileage logs for vehicle use
- Course descriptions and certificates for training
- Attendee and purpose notes for business meals
- Labour/material separation for kotitalousvähennys
- Records for at least 6 years where accounting rules require it

For kotitalousvähennys:
- Invoice must separate labour from materials.
- Company should be in the prepayment register.
- Only labour is deductible.

For vehicle expenses:
- Keep a mileage log.
- Separate business and private driving.
- Record purpose of each trip.

For tools:
- Keep receipt.
- Document work use percentage if mixed use.

GENERAL NON-DEDUCTIBLE EXPENSES
===============================

Usually non-deductible:
- Personal living expenses
- Private meals
- Normal commuting, except separate commuting deduction rules
- Private insurance
- Entertainment for personal guests
- Materials for kotitalousvähennys
- Devices/equipment under kotitalousvähennys
- Trade union membership fees from 2026
- New construction under kotitalousvähennys
- Expenses without documentation

FINAL NOTE
==========
These rules are for classification and preliminary deduction analysis.
Final deductibility depends on taxpayer type, income type, documentation,
invoice details and current Verohallinto guidance.
